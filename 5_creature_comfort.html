Creature Comfort

Creature Comfort is a collaborative photography project with Rebecca Leung and Bob Shi. Together, we built a life-sized papier-mâché head and took it on a journey through Singapore’s everyday spaces. Worn and performed by me, the oversized head becomes a gentle, awkward intruder—playing claw machines, sitting alone at hawker centres, waiting at traffic lights. Part absurd, part tender, the project reflects on visibility, vulnerability, and the quiet comedy of public life.
